# Family Connections & Relationships

This document maps out the family relationships and interpersonal connections within the S.E.A. universe.

## The Hightower Family

### Family Tree
```
Hightower Family Line
├── Harrison Hightower III (disappeared 1899)
│   └── Related to (cousins or brothers - exact relation unknown)
└── George Hightower (murdered 1877)
    └── Married to Constance Hatchaway (1877)
```

### Key Relationships

**Harrison Hightower III** ↔ **George Hightower**
- Relationship: Related (likely cousins or brothers)
- George was a wealthy world-traveler from Newport Beach
- Harrison was a corrupt S.E.A. member and collector in New York City
- Both met tragic ends: George was murdered by his wife, Harrison disappeared due to a curse

**George Hightower** ↔ **Constance Hatchaway**
- Relationship: Husband and wife (married 1877)
- Constance, the "Black Widow Bride," murdered George with an axe
- This was Constance's last known kill
- Constance inherited George's estate after his death
- They lived in an antebellum mansion in New Orleans

**Harrison Hightower III** ↔ **Shiriki Utundu**
- Relationship: Possessor and cursed
- Harrison stole the idol from the Mtundu tribe in 1899
- He mocked the curse, leading to his disappearance on December 31, 1899

### Locations Connected to Hightowers
- **Hotel Hightower** - New York City (Tower of Terror, Tokyo DisneySea)
- **Hightower Mansion** - New Orleans (Haunted Mansion lore)
- **Hightower Trust** - Organization that funded archaeological expeditions

## The Ravenswood Family

### Family Tree
```
Ravenswood Family
└── Henry Ravenswood (1795-1860, became The Phantom)
    └── Martha Ravenswood (1802-1860)
        └── Melanie Ravenswood (1842-1860)
            └── Suitors (all murdered by The Phantom):
                ├── Barry Claude
                ├── Sawyer Bottom
                ├── Captain Rowan D. Falls
                └── Ignatius "Iggy" Knight
```

### Key Relationships

**Henry Ravenswood** ↔ **Thunder Mesa / Big Thunder Mountain**
- Henry struck gold and founded the Thunder Mesa Mining Company
- Built a Victorian manor on Boot Hill overlooking Big Thunder Mountain
- In the Disneyland Paris version, he personally oversaw Barnabas T. Bullion's mining operations

**Henry Ravenswood** ↔ **Barnabas T. Bullion**
- In the Paris version: Henry oversaw Bullion's Big Thunder Mining operations
- In American versions: Bullion operates independently but connects to the same curse mythology

**Henry Ravenswood** (as The Phantom) ↔ **Melanie Ravenswood**
- Father-daughter relationship that became corrupted after death
- Henry's ghost prevented Melanie from marrying any suitors
- He murdered or caused "accidents" for all of Melanie's potential husbands
- His spirit appears behind her in her bridal portrait

**The Ravenswood Family** ↔ **1860 Earthquake**
- The earthquake killed the entire Ravenswood family
- Also killed the mayor and sheriff of Thunder Mesa
- Henry's spirit remained as The Phantom of Phantom Manor

### Locations Connected to Ravenswoods
- **Ravenswood Manor** - Thunder Mesa (Phantom Manor, Disneyland Paris)
- **Big Thunder Mountain** - Source of the family's wealth
- **Thunder Mesa** - Town founded by Henry

## The Falls Family

### Family Tree
```
Falls Family
└── Dr. Albert Falls (missing)
    └── [Unknown generation(s)]
        ├── Alberta Falls (current generation)
        └── [Unknown]
            └── Siobhan "Puffin" Murphy (distant cousin to Alberta)
```

### Key Relationships

**Dr. Albert Falls** ↔ **Alberta Falls**
- Relationship: Grandfather and granddaughter
- Dr. Falls was a prominent S.E.A. member who founded the Jungle Navigation Company
- He mysteriously disappeared years before the main storyline
- Alberta inherited the company and carries on his legacy
- In 1938, Alberta was president of the Jungle Navigation Company

**Alberta Falls** ↔ **Siobhan "Puffin" Murphy**
- Relationship: Distant cousins
- Both part of the extended Falls family

**Dr. Albert Falls** ↔ **Dr. Kon Chunosuke**
- Relationship: Fellow S.E.A. members and friends
- In 1938, Alberta Falls met Kon Chunosuke and recognized the S.E.A. logo on his equipment
- She invited him on a jungle cruise, honoring the connection between the two explorers

### Locations Connected to Falls Family
- **Jungle Navigation Company** - Founded by Dr. Albert Falls
- **Skipper Canteen** - Restaurant featuring Dr. Falls' legacy and S.E.A. references
- **Jungle Cruise** - The expedition rides operated by the Falls family business

## Rivalries & Conflicts

**Harrison Hightower III** ↔ **Mtundu Tribe**
- Hightower stole Shiriki Utundu despite tribal warnings
- The curse ultimately led to his disappearance

**Harrison Hightower III** ↔ **Temple Deities** (Raging Spirits)
- Hightower and his valet stole the water god's head
- The Hightower Trust later caused the fire and water gods to face each other
- This angered the deities, causing supernatural destruction

**Lord Henry Mystic** ↔ **Professor Garrett Reed**
- Mystic expelled Reed for unethical practices
- Reed sought the Emerald Trinity after expulsion

**The Phantom (Henry Ravenswood)** ↔ **Melanie's Suitors**
- The Phantom murdered all of Melanie's suitors:
  - Barry Claude
  - Sawyer Bottom
  - Captain Rowan D. Falls
  - Ignatius "Iggy" Knight

**Constance Hatchaway** ↔ **Her Husbands**
- Serial killer relationship
- George Hightower was her last known victim in 1877

**Barnabas T. Bullion** ↔ **The Thunderbird / Big Thunder Spirit**
- Bullion ignored warnings about the curse
- Continued mining despite supernatural threats
