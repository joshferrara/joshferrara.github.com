# S.E.A. Timeline

A chronological history of the Society of Explorers and Adventurers and its members.

## 1538

**August 12, 1538** - The Society of Explorers and Adventurers is established at Explorers Landing in Porto Paradiso, Italy. The Renaissance sailing ship becomes associated with the society's headquarters.

> **Location**: Fortress Explorations, Tokyo DisneySea

## 1795

**1795** - Henry Ravenswood is born. He would later strike gold in Big Thunder Mountain and found Thunder Mesa.

## 1802

**1802** - Martha Ravenswood (Henry's wife) is born.

## 1842

**1842** - Melanie Ravenswood (daughter of Henry and Martha) is born.

## 1848

**1848** - Barnabas T. Bullion receives a land grant to establish the Big Thunder Mining Company. The mines are personally overseen by Henry Ravenswood, who builds a Victorian manor on Boot Hill overlooking Big Thunder Mountain.

> **Location**: Big Thunder Mountain Railroad (Disneyland, Magic Kingdom, Disneyland Paris)

## 1851

**1851** - Jason Chandler serves as club secretary under President Vitale Robustelli. Vitale Robustelli officially brings in the first female S.E.A. member: Camellia Falco, an aviation pioneer.

## 1860

**1860** - A massive earthquake hits Thunder Mesa, sinking an entire portion of the town into a canyon and killing many residents, including the mayor, sheriff, and the Ravenswood family. Henry Ravenswood's spirit becomes the Phantom of Phantom Manor.

> **Location**: Phantom Manor, Disneyland Paris

## 1877

**1877** - Constance Hatchaway marries George Hightower, a wealthy world-traveler from Newport Beach (and relative of Harrison Hightower III). They move to an antebellum mansion in New Orleans. Constance murders George with an axe, inheriting his estate.

> **Location**: Haunted Mansion (Disneyland, Magic Kingdom, Tokyo Disneyland)

## 1880s

**c. 1880** - Jason Chandler is elected president of the Society of Explorers and Adventurers.

**1882** - Jason Chandler embarks on a riverboat expedition in British Columbia.

## Early 1900s

**c. 1900** - Harrison Hightower III becomes a corrupt member of S.E.A., collecting treasures and antiquities through shady means. He builds the Hotel Hightower in New York City.

**1899** - Harrison Hightower III steals the cursed idol Shiriki Utundu from the Mtundu tribe in the Congo Basin, Africa, despite warnings from tribal members.

> **Location**: Tower of Terror, Tokyo DisneySea

**December 31, 1899** - During a New Year's Eve party at Hotel Hightower, Harrison Hightower mocks the curse of Shiriki Utundu by extinguishing his cigar on the idol's head. At midnight, green lightning strikes the hotel, and Hightower disappears in the elevator. His screams are heard, but he is never seen again.

> **Location**: Tower of Terror, Tokyo DisneySea

**December 5, 1901** - Captain Mary Oceaneer discovers a golden sword during a deep sea dive. She brings her parrot Salty along in a custom diving suit.

> **Location**: Miss Adventure Falls, Typhoon Lagoon; Disney Cruise Line Oceaneer Lab

## 1907+

**After 1907** - Jason Chandler and French aviator Captain Brieux map regions of the Mekong River from Brieux's airship, The Hyperion.

## 1921

**1921** - Merriweather Adam Pleasure builds the Adventurers Club on Pleasure Island to house his collection and entertain fellow explorers. It becomes his personal S.E.A. chapter.

> **Location**: Adventurers Club (defunct), Walt Disney World Pleasure Island

## 1930s

**1938** - Dr. Kon Chunosuke, a Japanese entomologist and S.E.A. member, travels to the jungle seeking insect specimens. He meets Alberta Falls, president of the Jungle Navigation Company and granddaughter of Dr. Albert Falls (a fellow S.E.A. member who had gone missing). Their expedition goes awry when skipper Felix Pechman XIII crashes their boat, stranding them in the African veldt where they're chased up a pole by a rhinoceros.

**1938** - Jock Lindsay (Indiana Jones' pilot) and Indiana Jones stumble upon Disney Springs while chasing a mythology-based tip in central Florida. Jock buys waterfront property and eventually settles down, piloting seaplane tours. He builds a hangar, air tower, and runway, inviting fellow S.E.A. members to visit.

> **Location**: Jock Lindsey's Hangar Bar, Disney Springs

## Modern Era (Real-World Theme Park Openings)

**1989** - The Adventurers Club opens at Walt Disney World's Pleasure Island, serving as the inspiration for S.E.A.

**2001** - Fortress Explorations debuts on opening day at Tokyo DisneySea, serving as the society's original headquarters.

**2006** - Tower of Terror opens at Tokyo DisneySea, introducing Harrison Hightower III to the lore.

**2008** - "The Leonardo Challenge" scavenger hunt program begins at Fortress Explorations.

**2013** - Mystic Manor opens at Hong Kong Disneyland, introducing Lord Henry Mystic and his monkey Albert. Harrison Hightower III is retroactively established as an S.E.A. member.

**2015** - Jungle Skipper Canteen restaurant opens at Magic Kingdom, founded by Dr. Albert Falls. Multiple S.E.A. members are referenced throughout the restaurant.

**2015** - Jock Lindsay's Hangar Bar debuts at Disney Springs, establishing Jock's connection to S.E.A.

**2017** - Miss Adventure Falls debuts at Typhoon Lagoon, introducing Captain Mary Oceaneer.

**2018** - Tropical Hideaway opens at Disneyland, featuring expedition paddles from S.E.A. members.

**2019** - Soaring: Fantastic Flight opens at Tokyo DisneySea, featuring Camellia Falco's Museum of Fantastic Flight.

**2021** - Jungle Cruise receives a major update introducing Alberta Falls as the current owner of the Jungle Navigation Company, carrying on her grandfather Dr. Albert Falls' legacy.

## Undated Events

- Lord Henry Mystic rescues his monkey companion Albert from a giant spider, and they become inseparable.
- Lord Henry Mystic acquires an enchanted music box that brings his entire collection to life.
- Harrison Hightower III and his valet Smelding steal the head of the water god idol from Raging Spirits temple, angering the fire and water deities.
- The Hightower Trust accidentally restores a temple so fire and water gods face each other, causing supernatural destruction.
- Captain Mary Oceaneer's ship and treasure collection are caught in a rogue typhoon and scattered across what becomes Typhoon Lagoon.
- Professor Garrett Reed is expelled from S.E.A. by Lord Henry Mystic for using questionable tactics to obtain archaeological artifacts. He later seeks the Emerald Trinity.
- Madame Zarkov operates the Museum of the Weird and advises Jason Chandler about supernatural occurrences at Big Thunder Mountain, warning Barnabas T. Bullion about the Thunderbird curse.
