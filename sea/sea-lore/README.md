# Disney's Society of Explorers and Adventurers (S.E.A.) Lore Database

This directory contains a comprehensive database of the lore, characters, and connections within Disney's Society of Explorers and Adventurers universe.

## What is S.E.A.?

The Society of Explorers and Adventurers (S.E.A.) is a fictional organization that serves as a shared universe connecting various Disney park attractions around the world. Founded on August 12, 1538, at Explorers Landing in Porto Paradiso (now the site of Fortress Explorations at Tokyo DisneySea), the society comprises scientists, artists, researchers, inventors, and adventurers from around the world.

## Founding Principles

The S.E.A. crest features symbols representing four guiding principles:
- **Romance** - The spirit of adventure and discovery
- **Adventure** - Bold exploration of unknown territories
- **Discovery** - Scientific and cultural findings
- **Innovation** - Technological advancement and creativity
