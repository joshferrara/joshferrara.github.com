FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Is AI Week suitable for beginners? +

Absolutely. Each track includes beginner, intermediate, and advanced sessions clearly labeled in the program. The "Just Talking to My Robot" track is specifically designed for people with no technical background.

What's the refund policy? +

Full refunds are available until March 22, 2026. After that, passes can be transferred to another attendee at no charge. Contact hello@aiweekbr.com for transfers.

Will sessions be recorded? +

Yes. Full Week and VIP pass holders receive access to the complete recording library within 48 hours of each session. Day Pass holders receive recordings for their selected day only.

Do I need to bring my own laptop for workshops? +

Yes, for the SDLC and Robot tracks. The Laser Engraving track provides all equipment on-site. Workshop-specific requirements will be emailed to registered attendees one week before the event.

Is there a Code of Conduct? +

Yes. AI Week is committed to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. Our full Code of Conduct is available on the registration page and will be enforced throughout the event.

Can I attend just the hackathon? +

The hackathon is included with Full Week and VIP passes. We also offer a limited number of Hackathon-Only passes for $149 — check back in March for availability.

Ready to build what's next?

Early bird pricing ends March 15. Join 1,200 builders, makers, and dreamers in Baton Rouge.
